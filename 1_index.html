Know how the validation works

  • Step 1: Upload Voter's EPIC number Evaluator uploads their username and password if they exists in our database. Then the system extracts and verifies the evaluator and takes to the voter authentication process.
  • Step 2: Validate Voter Details The extracted details (name, Father name, city, etc.) are cross-checked against the official election database. If discrepancies are found, users will be notified to re-upload or correct their details.
  • Step 3: Election Day Authentication On the election day, verified evaluator log in and authenticate themselves. The system confirms eligibility and prevents duplicate voting.
  • Step 4: Secure Voting Access Once authenticated, eligible voters gain access to the voting system. Their vote is securely cast and recorded in compliance with election security standards.
  • Step 5: Confirmation & Security Measures A confirmation message is provided after a successful vote. Advanced encryption and security measures ensure data privacy and prevent fraud.
